# sales-dashboard-using-excel
A comprehensive sales analytics dashboard built in Excel, analyzing the Sample Superstore dataset to provide actionable insights into sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across the United States.

# Project Overview
This interactive dashboard delivers a complete view of retail operations, featuring key performance indicators, regional analysis, and product category insights to support data-driven business decisions.

# Key Features
Dynamic KPI Cards: Real-time tracking of Total Sales ($885K), Total Profit ($210K), Profit Margin (23.78%), and operational metrics
Regional Analysis: Geographic breakdown with the West region leading sales performance
Category Performance: Detailed insights across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology segments
Customer Segmentation: Revenue analysis showing Consumer segment as the primary driver ($450K+)
Profitability Insights: Top sub-categories and discount impact analysis
Interactive Filters: Region and Category slicers for dynamic data exploration

# Tools & Techniques
Data Cleaning: Transformed raw data to ensure accuracy and consistency
Power Query: Advanced data transformation and ETL processes
Power Pivot: Created calculated measures and data model relationships
Pivot Tables & Charts: Built comprehensive analytical views
Dashboard Design: Developed an intuitive, visually appealing interface with coordinated color schemes

# Key Insights
West region dominates with the largest market share and strategic growth opportunity
Consumer segment generates over $450K in sales, significantly outpacing other segments
Binders lead sub-category profitability at $40K+
Average shipping time of 3.97 days maintains operational efficiency
Technology and Office Supplies show strong profit margins

# Dataset
Sample Superstore dataset containing sales transactions, customer information, and product details across multiple U.S. regions.
